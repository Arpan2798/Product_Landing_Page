Voice Augmented Assistive Kit

This device aims at minimizing the communication gap between the mute/dumb and the general public.

Let's see how it works!

The mute/dumb people talk in sign language talk in sign language. Not many people know sign language. Thus there is communication gap between these two groups of people. Our device interprets the finger motion of mute people and analyze it and finally converts it to an alphabet which an automated voice speaks so that common people can listen and understand what they are trying to speak.
